To Whom it may concern:

I am writing this as a small token of gratitude and appreciation.

My wife and I have lived in our North Las Vegas home for only about 18 months. this spring as the weather started to warm up, we noticed that our air conditioning was not working the way that it should, and we decided that we needed to contact a professional to come take a look at it.

I called your company last week after seeing the number while driving behind one of your service vehicles on the freeway. I gave a brief description of my concerns to the very polite woman that answered the phone, although I regretfully did not get her name. She assured me that she was certain that she could find a time to send out a technician that worked my schedule. I work graveyard hours, and this limits when I am available during the week. After a few minutes, she said that she would have someone out to take a look that same afternoon.

Eddie arrived at my home right on time later that day. He was professional, and respectful. In spite of my being a bit younger than he, he diagnosed the problem with my system in minutes and quickly got to work fixing them. He took the time to explain exactly what he was doing, and even answered several questions. When he was finished I asked if he thought it was worth taking a look at the equipment in the attic. Even though he surely could have charged extra, he stated that he was confident that he had fixed our issues. He even went so far as to give me a card, and explain that he lived in the area, telling me to call if there were any other concerns about our system. When all was said and done I found the final price of the service and repair surprisingly affordable.

It was so refreshing to know that even in such a city as Las Vegas, with our easy money, and get mine attitude, that there are still honest companies like yours with honest employees like Eddie. Thank you so much for putting "doing the right thing" above the bottom line.

Homeowner, Las Vegas
