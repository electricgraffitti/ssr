With the ever rising cost in energy, and warmer weather, knowing how to lower your utility bills is important for every homeowner and business owner.  One of the most effective ways you can save money in the long run is to have Silver State Refrigeration install a more efficient running air conditioning system on your home or place of business.

For homeowners looking to upgrade or replace their existing air conditioning system for a more energy efficient one, there is good news.  The U.S. Department of Energy is now setting new requirements for all air conditioning systems to be manufactured at a minimum of (13) – Seasonal Energy Efficiency Rating or (SEER).  This new government regulation is estimated to save over $19 billion in future energy costs by the year 2030, enough energy to light all U.S. cities for 20 years.  (13) – SEER air conditioners are by far more efficient that the majority of air conditioners in use in homes and business today.

Several manufactures’ products offer improved efficiency and comfort compared to older units that are being used.  Some manufactures produce a full lineup of high efficient cooling products, including (13) – SEER through (18) – SEER products.  Visit our products page and visit some of our manufacturers that we use in our services and let us help you decide which replacement unit can provide you with improved comfort, air quality, and energy savings.
