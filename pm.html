Heating, cooling, refrigeration, and air flow systems work incredibly hard to perform the functions for your home or business.  The constant starting, stopping and continuous operation can wear down your equipment if upkeep and inspections are not performed on a regular basis.

   
 
     As a qualified service provider we know that preventative maintenance can reduce energy consumption, increase equipment life, reduce downtime and help avoid costly repairs.  The following is a conformance checklist that we perform for each type of equipment covered under a P.M. contract, so you can be assured your equipment is running efficiently as possible. Contact us today and let us help make sure you get the most out of your equipment.

 PREVENTATIVE MAINTENANCE CONFORMANCE SHEET

Air conditioning group__________________________________________

  • Condenser clean
  • Belts replaced or adjusted
  • Condensate line clear and draining
  • Store at proper temperature
  • Filters replaced
  • Fresh air screen cleaned
  • All panels secure
  • All trash removed from roof
  • Supply & return air grills clean
  • Inspect blower wheels for rotation and cleanliness
  • Visual inspection of equipment
  • Check all electrical

 Ice maker_____________________________________________________

  • Condenser clean
  • Water filter replaced
  • Scale removed from unit
  • Media filter installed
  • Water leaks

 Refrigeration group_____________________________________________

  • Condenser clean
  • Evaporator clean
  • Case temperature at proper temperature
  • All fans operating
  • Media filter installed
  • Door gaskets o.k.
  • Door hardware o.k.
  • Fan guards clean

 Exhaust & Make up air__________________________________________

  • Belts replaced or adjusted for proper tension
  • Bearings lubed
  • Debris cleaned from hood
  • Swamp pads in proper shape
  • Float adjusted
  • Make up air screen clean
