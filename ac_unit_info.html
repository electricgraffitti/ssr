Something To Consider:

Look for the ENERGY STAR label when replacing your old inefficient air conditioner.  ENERGY STAR appliances utilize superior technology, which in the long run require less energy and less money to operate. Most of the leading manufacturers offer ENERGY STAR air conditioners.  Just look for the label.

The Right Size Matters:

A system that is too large will cool or heat your home or business to rapidly, but you still may not feel comfortable.  A system that starts and stops in this manner can shorten the overall life of your equipment and increase you heating and cooling bills.  On the flip side to this is a system that is too small and just cannot get the job done.  This air conditioner will run constantly which will also decrease the units overall life and increase your utility bills.
